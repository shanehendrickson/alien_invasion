Player controls ship at bottom of screen.
Player can move ship left and right. Arrow Keys. A & D?
Player can shoot bullets. Spacebar.
At start, fleet of alien ships fills sky and moves across and down screen.
Player shoots to destroy enemies.
If all aliens destroyed, new fleet appears: faster than previous.
If alien hits player ship or reaches bottom of screen, player loses life.
If player loses three lives, game ends.
